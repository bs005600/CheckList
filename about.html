About the Designer

Brooke Saab

Designer of Checklist

As a full-time college student I understand the struggle of seemingly never ending to-do-lists. Between working multiple jobs to make ends meet, towering assignments, and the whirlwind of young adulthood, when is there time to even manage your time? Next to never, my own necessity and that of my peers inspired me to design a self-planning interface with the advanced tools technology can provide but the simple feel and aesthetics of a handwritten to-do list. I hope CheckList can create peace-of-mind for those who may be struggling with their entrance to young adulthood or any stage of life where managing your time and energy is seemingly impossible.
